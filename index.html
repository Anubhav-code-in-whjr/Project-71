The Heritage School, Kolkata is spread across 9.5 acres in the Kolkata suburb of Anandapur and is beautifully landscaped. Five blocks house classrooms, science and computer labs while another block called the Abhivyakti where different activities like yoga, singing and dancing are taught. The school campus also hosts a football, cricket and a volleyball field. Other facilities include a rock climbing area, archery, rifle, shooting range, swimming pool, basketball and tennis courts, and a gymnasium. There are two dining halls and a canteen. The campus also houses an auditorium and an audio-visual room.

What's New
The Heritage School turns a tram into an art gallery

The Heritage School recently showed what the fusion of art and innovation can look like. The students of The Heritage School, in association with West Bengal Publishers Coordination Committee, organised Gallery on Wheels -- an art gallery inside a tram where they exhibited art, pottery, photography and textile. The exhibition-cum-sale was inaugurated by Rajanvir Singh Kapur, the managing director of West Bengal Transport Corporation (WBTC). For two days, the tram travelled along various routes and stopped by the Nonapukur, Esplanade, Shyambazar and Gariahat tram depots. The idea behind the exhibition was to cherish the heritage of Bengal, especially Kolkata. The students of The Heritage School -- from Class I to XII -- along with the teachers presented photographs, pottery and textiles they had made.

Young Chef Olympiad IIHM

Seven students of class 7, from The Heritage School, qualified for Young Chef Olympiad organised by IIHM on 19th November, 2019. They had passed the passed the preliminary round held earlier at the school campus. It was a novel experience for these budding chefs as it was their first culinary experience in a professional kitchen and the students had a great learning experience.

Bipaasha won Gold in East India Karate Championship


Bipaasha Paul, a student of The Heritage School, was selected to represent Karate Association of Bengal for the East India Karate Championship 2020 held at Bhubaneswar on 24th January.She made everyone proud by winning Gold (Sub-junior Kata) and is selected for the upcoming Nationals at Delhi.
